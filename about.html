Artistic History:

I have loved art for as long as I can remember. When I was a kid all I would ask for, for Christmas was art and craft supplies. I took art as soon as it was an option in Grade 7 up to grade 12. But more recently it has been a constant cycle of hyperfocusing on some creative craft for a week, only to fall off and not touch it again for months. This has been a real struggle for me, because I want nothing more to improve and refine my craft, but I get frustrated at my lack of quick progress only to just quit.

Some of my the creative mediums I use are:

  • Painting
  • Sketching
  • Crochet
  • Collage
  • Photography
