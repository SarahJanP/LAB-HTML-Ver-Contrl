About Me!

Artist History:

I have loved art for as long as I can remember, when I was a kid all I would ask for Christmas was art and craft supplies, I
took art as soon as it was an option in Grade 7 up to grade 12. I have always loved art but it has been a constant cycle of
hyperfocusing on some creative craft for a week, only to fall off and not touch it again for months. This has been a real struggle
for me, because I want nothing more to improve and refine my craft, but I get frustrated at my lack of quick progress only to just quit.
